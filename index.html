About us

Built on the same idea as our name

Friends Vidyanikethan High School brings students, teachers and families together around one goal: an education built on trust and partnership. Our emblem — two hands, one circle — is a reminder that the school runs on everyone pulling in the same direction.

This page is the everyday front door to that partnership: every system the school runs, gathered in one place, so no one has to remember five different links.
