About us

Built on the same idea as our name

Friends Vidyanikethan High School, run by Friends Educational Society in Maddikera, Kurnool, brings students, teachers and families together around one goal: an education built on trust and partnership. Our emblem — two hands, one circle — is a reminder that the school runs on everyone pulling in the same direction.

Our vision is to nurture confident, capable learners who carry strong values into the world. Our mission is to combine academic excellence with a genuinely friendly, supportive campus, where every student gets individual attention.

  • Academic excellence
  • Friendly environment
  • Discipline with care
  • Respect & moral values
  • Every child included
  • Community & partnership

Admissions

Joining Friends Vidyanikethan

  1. Enquiry

    Contact the school office in person, by phone or through the enquiry button below.

  2. Campus visit

    Visit the school to see the facilities and meet the staff.

  3. Application form

    Collect or download the admission form and fill in the student's details.

  4. Document submission

    Submit the completed form along with the required documents to the office.

  5. Confirmation

    The office confirms the seat and shares fee and joining details.

Eligibility

  • Age-appropriate for the class applied for (exact age criteria to be added)
  • Seats subject to availability in each class

Documents required

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records / transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Aadhar card copy
  • Address proof
